BINGO

Welcome to the Bingo simulator! Write the names of the participants separated by commas (,), put the size of the boards (3x3, 4x4, 5x5) and how many turns do you want to play (25-50).

Rules:

  • If a player fills his board the game will end
  • The player with the most points wins
  • Horizontal line earns you 1 point
  • Vertical line earns you 1 point
  • Diagonal line earns you 3 points
  • Full board earns you 5 extra points
